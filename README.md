## Project

With a reported 8.8 million arrivals to our shores in 2017 according to the Australian Bureau of Statistics it is safe to say the Australian tourism market is a hot market. With dramatic growth since 2012 there is a large market of overseas visitors arriving daily with phones in hands and cash to spend in local restaurants, bars and attractions. My goal is to develop an app which partners with local businesses to provide discounted meals, drinks and activities to visitors in the Australian major capital cities.

The app "Tabula Rasa" will direct overseas visitors to partner businesses providing discounts on food, tours and experiences. The businesses will gain not only targeted customers but will be able to purchase analytics about the customers in order to influence their future decisions and marketing campaigns. These businesses will also have an opportunity to market thier venue within the app via banners and pop ups.

Tabula Rasa will be a simple and clean app. The app will be brokendown into the following sections:

- Attractions/Experiences
- Dining
- Bars and nightlife
- Meetups

Prior to launch we will target local businesses we believe can be succesful partners and offer initial free advertising of their service in exchange for discounts for our users. We will work to offer compounded deals ie. Dinner and a movie to multiply exposure. An important aspect of the app will be using location services to show nearby deals and targeted advertising. In addition to the local deals we would like to offer a space where travellers can find others like themselves and "meetup" to take advantage of group discounts. 

Conversely our business partners will gain valuable insights into customer trends and demographics as vistiors begin to redeem the discounts. Not only will foot traffic be directed at them throught the app generating revenue but they will be able to make better decisons in the future based on the analytics gained.

After an initial consultation period to gauge interest we will need to develop a beta version of the app for testing. We will select a pool of test users to use the app and provide feedback in order to measure ease of use, functionality and desirability. If successful we will be required to create developer accounts for android/iphone so that we may publish the app. Once fully tested we will be able to integrate analytics to track user profiles and engagement as well as retention of the app. After a base of customers has been accrued and retained we will be able to monetize features on the app as well as promote ourselves and our partners within the app. 

For our initial rollout we will be using a web based app developing tool. After guaging interest it may or may not be worthwhile to invest in hiring a developer to publish Tabula Rasa.
